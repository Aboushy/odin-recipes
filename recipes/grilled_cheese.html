Grilled Cheese

Description

A grilled cheese, sometimes known as a toasted sandwich or cheese toastie, 
            is a hot cheese sandwich typically prepared by heating slices of cheese 
            between slices of bread with a cooking fat such as butter or mayonnaise on a frying pan, 
            griddle, or sandwich toaster, until the bread browns and the cheese melts.




Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheddar Cheese Slices
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Grate cheese
  2. Butter
  3. Head start fry
  4. Flip bread & cheese it
