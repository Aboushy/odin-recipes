Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken parmesan or chicken parmigiana is a dish that consists of breaded chicken breast covered in tomato sauce and mozzarella, 
            Parmesan, or provolone. Ham or bacon is sometimes added. The dish originated in the Italian diaspora in the United States during the early 20th century.




Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast plate halves
  • Kosher salt
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • 1 Egg, beaten

Directions

  1. Prep Marinara
  2. Cut 2 chicken breasts
  3. Set up 3 shallow bowls
  4. Coat chicken
  5. Saute
  6. Assemble
